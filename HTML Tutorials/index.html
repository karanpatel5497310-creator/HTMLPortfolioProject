ABOUT VIRAT KOHLI


Virat Kohli a renowned Indian cricketer celebrated for his aggressive batting, exceptional leadership, and numerous world records, who rose to prominence after captaining India to victory in the 2008 U-19 World Cup. A versatile and consistent player across all formats of the game, he has captained the Indian national team and is widely considered one of the greatest batsmen of all time, inspiring millions globally through his passion, discipline, and commitment to fitness.
